Background on qbio.ca seminars


The intention is to have talks twice a year with two invited (20min+10min Q&A) and several contributed talks (10+5min). The theme is biological physics, and we intend to have at least one talk  from a Canadian group every time. We aim to have a diversity of speakers, identities, perspectives, and topics. Email andrew.rutenberg@me.com or aidan.brown@torontomu.ca with suggestions about talks you'd like to see and speakers you'd like to hear, to volunteer for a contributed talk, or if you'd like to be removed from the list.


Please register at  https://forms.gle/isDZ7Es577b8hSeN6, if you haven’t already. All are welcome (free)!
